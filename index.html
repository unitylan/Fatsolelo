About Us

About Us

Fatsolelo is a consulting organization with over 20 years of full-cycle Human Resource Management experience. We pride ourselves on providing tailored HR solutions to businesses of all sizes, focusing on practical and efficient strategies to meet your organizational needs.

Our services are designed to support your HR functions while ensuring compliance, development, and growth for your business.

HR Services

HR Consulting

As an experienced HR Consultant, I review and update your current employment documentation to ensure that it is fit-for-purpose for your business and keeps you in line with ever-changing legislation. I also provide hands-on support to help you manage your everyday people's issues. The services offered include:

  • HR-related advice and support for recruitment
  • Employee relations
  • Disciplinary and grievance issues
  • Staff issues
  • Redundancy processes
  • Documentation development and management
  • Development and implementation of performance appraisal systems

Strategic Planning

Fatsolelo helps organisations develop and monitor effective HR strategic plans by aligning vision, goals, and objectives with practical action steps. We provide ongoing HR support and structural analysis to ensure your business meets its strategic targets successfully. Key services include:

  • Appraising vision, goals, and objectives
  • Creating a strategic HR roadmap
  • Providing continuous HR support during change
  • Analyzing current organisational structures
  • Developing plans to meet strategic objectives
  • Designing actionable plans to achieve outcomes

Internal Audit

We help organizations prepare for HR audits, which ensure compliance with labor and health regulations. HR audits help identify compliance issues and establish best practices. Key services include:

  • Department of health private hospital audit checklist
  • Department of labor audit checklist
  • Core-standard audit checklist

Labour Solutions

Labor Dispute Resolution

Fatsolelo offers labour dispute mediation and alternative dispute resolution (ADR) services—including conciliation and arbitration—as a more effective and cost-efficient alternative to traditional legal processes. Our structured approach helps parties identify issues, explore solutions, and reach mutually acceptable agreements. In contrast to the strained and often delayed processes at the CCMA—characterised by a legalistic approach and declining settlement rates—Fatsolelo's services provide timely, practical, and resolution-focused outcomes.

Payroll & Benefits

We manage the full payroll process in compliance with SARS regulations and Labour Law. This includes processing payroll runs, calculating statutory deductions such as PAYE, UIF, and SDL, and issuing payslips and IRP5s. We handle the monthly submission of EMP201 forms and facilitate payments through eFiling. In line with the Skills Development Act, we ensure the correct calculation and submission of the Skills Development Levy (SDL) for employers with annual payrolls exceeding R500,000. Additionally, we submit all required monthly reports and UI19 declarations on your behalf, ensuring full statutory compliance.

Employment Equity

It's that time of the year when employers must submit their Employment Equity reports to the Department of Labour—a critical process for identifying transformation gaps and implementing affirmative action measures. Fatsolelo supports organisations in preparing and submitting these annual reports efficiently, ensuring full compliance with regulatory requirements ahead of deadlines. In addition, we assist with developing a Leadership Development strategy that strengthens your transformation efforts and positions your leadership team for long-term success—meeting both compliance goals and business objectives.
